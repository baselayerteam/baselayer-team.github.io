FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

How long does a website take to build?

Starter sites are ready within 48 hours. Standard and Growth packages take 3–5 days depending on how quickly you send us content and feedback.

What's included in the design process?

We handle everything — design, content, setup and publishing. You just tell us about your business and send your logo if you have one. No tech skills needed at all.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. We build everything and you can reach us on WhatsApp anytime you have a question or need an update.

How much does a website or business tool cost?

Websites start from R2,500 and business tools from R2,000 once-off. The Starter package is once-off with no monthly fee. All other packages include a mandatory monthly retainer starting from R900/month — this covers ongoing support, updates, and maintenance so your product always works.

Why is the monthly retainer mandatory?

We include a retainer on every package because your business depends on your digital tools working reliably. The retainer covers ongoing support, updates, bug fixes, and direct WhatsApp access — so you're never left without help when you need it.

Can you help if I already have a website?

Yes. We can redesign, rebuild, or improve what you have. Many businesses come to us to make their sites faster, more mobile-friendly, or easier to use.
